Production

Advanced Kiln Technology for Superior Performance

Production is the heart of DLH LIME’s operations. We boast superior manufacturing capabilities through our two state-of-the-art facilities, which operate a total of five high-capacity, modern kilns. This significant investment in industrial infrastructure allows us to provide a comprehensive portfolio of products, including Lump Lime, Powder Lime, Quicklime (CaO), Hydrated Lime (Ca(OH)2), and Burnt Dolomite.

The DLH LIME advantage lies in our integration of world-class production technologies:

  • PCC-Quality Lime Kiln Technology: Specifically engineered to produce high-activity Quicklime with exceptional purity, meeting the rigorous demands of the paper, chemical, and food processing industries.
  • Advanced Burnt Dolomite Kilns: Featuring precise thermal control systems to ensure stable physical and chemical properties, essential for metallurgy and glass manufacturing.
  • Modern Calcium Hydroxide (Ca(OH)2) Lines: Utilizing high-performance separation equipment to create ultra-fine, high-whiteness hydrated lime with superior solubility for environmental and water treatment applications.
